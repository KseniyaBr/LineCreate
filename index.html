Story about us

While many people think of apps as things that younger people use, there are all kinds of senior citizens who are very tech-savvy, and are using computers and mobile devices every day. Why not have an app that is specifically targeted towards them?

We are a company that produces new way to share photos by combining a dedicated team with ease of use, and ideal for older people. We called it LineCreate!
LineCreate is an app that makes it easy to step-up your photo editing game, make amazing images and share with friends.
LineCreate lets you be truly creative with its almost limitless features ― and it’s starting a movement to help the people “go beyond the filter” and make awesome pictures.
LineCreate can be used offline and implements new creative techniques.
